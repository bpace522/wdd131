Site Plan - Liger Energy

Site Name

Liger Energy - Your Batteries, Your Life - This name was chosen to reflec the goal of the company which is to return energy independence to the american citizen

Optional domain: ligerenergy.com

Site Purpose

This site is a resource for people that are trying to find more information on solar generators and 12V batteries

Scenarios

  • Which 12V battery should I use with a 200w solar panel for an off-grid cabin?
  • Can I use a solar generator to run my refrigerator during a power outage?

  • Secondary Color: Sky Blue #00BFFF – used for links, accents, and hover effects
  • Background: Light Beige #FAF9F6
  • Text: Dark Gray #333333

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat
